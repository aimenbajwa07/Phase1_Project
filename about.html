Who are We?


We are a group of random people from all ages, phases of life and industries. All of us are different except one common attribute that is "OUR LOVE OF AWESOME BOOKS". Have you recently read a book which you loved. You couldn't put it down. It made you feel happy, excited, anxious, sad, curious, exhilerated, etc. Did it change the way look at the world and people. Did it bring you even a pinch of satisfaction and happiness?

If any of the above actions applies to you, you are in the right place. Share your book with out people and spread that awesomeness. Help people feel the same thing you did when you read the book. It doesn't have to be a certain genre it can be about anything. It could be fiction, non-fiction, thriller, mystery, romantic, dystopian, science fiction, etc. The options are unlimited!!!! At the end of the day we're a group of chill people who want to bring other people joy by sharing and reading awesome books. :)
